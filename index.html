About the Artist

WHO
AM I?

I'm Kshitij. Been shooting since 2019, when a camera somehow became the most honest way I knew to say something.

Since then, I've had the chance to work with brands, shoot weddings, capture cars, style food, document podcasts, and make reels. If it's worth remembering, I want to be the one with the camera.

I also shoot on film. Some moments feel better with a little grain and no undo button.
